Biography

Albert Einstein is regarded to be one of the most important scientists in his field. Born in Germany, He developed the Theory of Relativity and made important discoveries to quantum mechanics as well. Albert Einstein changed our understanding of physics by developing the theory, E=mc^2. The theroy of relativity, which is the the formula for mass energy equivalence, is considerd to be "the world's most famous equation".
